Project Space

A place for students to expose themselves and gain experience by indulging in freelance projects


What you learn from school is just not enough to get into the competitive job market we have today. That is why we created a place where students can find freelance projects they can complete while they are in school. By engaging in these projects, you will not only gain experience but you will also get the chance to network with people and create a solid resume for yourself.

Find freelance projects

Browse through the pool of projects and choose one that best interests you. All projects are categorized by department. For instance, if you are a computer science major you can view projects posted in the engineering department. Most projects will be paid and in some instances they may require more than one person to complete. If you have questions, you can either post a public question or contact the poster directly.

Post your own project or idea

Everyone is welcome to post their ideas and projects. You can describe the requirements, deadlines, how many students you need and also supply them any relevant documents.

Find skilled students

You have the luxury of finding valuable, skilled students from our website. Students who would like to be contacted will have public profiles with detailed information about their experience, skill set and education. Type in the skill you are looking for and you will be presented with all the students that matches the criteria.

Create a student profile

Put your self out there and see how many opportunities you will get. Create your profile with information about your education, experience and skills. Others will contact you for their projects and even post reviews on your profile on how well you performed.
